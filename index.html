🎯 Our Story

We're eight high school students who experienced the same struggle—boring study materials, no real progress tracking, and feeling disconnected while learning. So we decided to build the solution ourselves. Etherva is born from our frustration and fueled by our passion to create an interactive platform that actually makes learning engaging, trackable, and social. We're building this for ourselves, and for every student who deserves better.
